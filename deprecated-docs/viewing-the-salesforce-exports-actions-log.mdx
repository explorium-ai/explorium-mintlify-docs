---
title: "ExactInsights: Viewing The Integration Actions Log"
description: "To view the log of exports into salesforce"
---

<Steps>
    <Step>
    Click on "CRM Integrations" in your profile menu.
    </Step>
    <Step>
    Navigate to "Integrations Activity Log" in the CRM Integrations screen.
    </Step>
    <Step>
    Each Export activity will be logged, noting the user, time, number of process rows and overall status of the export.
    </Step>
    <Step>
    Export status:

   1. "Running" - Export is in progress
   2. "Done" - All records were exported successfully
   3. "Partial Success" - Some of the records were exported successfully. Click to view error details.
   4. "Failed" - None of the records were exported. Click to view more details about the errors.
