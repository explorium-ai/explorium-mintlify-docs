## Core Capabilities

The API is designed to streamline data enrichment and business research. It supports:

- **Business Enrichments** – Retrieve firmographic, technographic, financial, and operational data about companies worldwide.
- **Prospect Enrichments** – Enhance lead data with contact details, social media presence, and professional history.
- **Bulk Enrichments** – Process up to 50 business or prospect records in a single request, improving efficiency.
- **Event Tracking** – Stay informed with real-time business and prospect events, such as job changes and funding rounds.
- **Autocomplete & Matching** – Improve entity resolution through fuzzy search and high-accuracy matching algorithms.

## Best Practices

- **Use Bulk Requests** – Process up to 50 records in a single request to improve efficiency.
- **Filter Wisely** – Choose filters with strong data coverage to ensure comprehensive results.
- **Monitor API Usage** – Track rate limits and credit consumption to avoid service disruptions.
- **Leverage Correlation IDs** – Each response includes a correlation ID for debugging and support.
- **Combine Endpoints** – Match businesses first, then use their Business IDs for deeper enrichment.

## Rate Limits & Error Handling

- **Rate Limits** – The API enforces per-second and per-minute limits to ensure stability.
- **Error Codes** – Standard HTTP error codes are returned for invalid requests or exceeded limits.
- **Logging & Debugging** – Responses contain correlation IDs for easy issue tracking.
