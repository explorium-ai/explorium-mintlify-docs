## Overview

### **Introduction**

The **Businesses Enrichments** API suite provides a comprehensive set of **data enrichment capabilities** that empower organizations to extract **deep business insights**. These enrichments allow businesses to **augment, analyze, and track** company information across various domains, including **firmographics, financials, workforce trends, market positioning, and website content changes**.

By leveraging Explorium’s **global datasets and AI-driven insights**, users can:

* Enhance **lead scoring and segmentation**.
* Track **company growth, funding, and workforce shifts**.
* Analyze **competitive landscapes and market trends**.
* Monitor **website content updates and keyword presence**.

This document provides an **overview of all available enrichments**, their use cases, and best practices for **maximizing business intelligence.**

## **How to Use Businesses Enrichments**

1. **Obtain a Business ID:** Use the **Match Businesses API** to retrieve a unique identifier for the target company.
2. **Select the Desired Enrichment:** Choose the enrichment endpoint that aligns with your business needs.
3. **Submit an API Request:** Send a `POST` request with the `business_id` to retrieve relevant data.
4. **Analyze and Act:** Integrate the enriched data into your workflows for advanced decision-making.

### **Best Practices**

* **Combine multiple enrichments** for a holistic company profile.
* **Use historical insights** to track trends over time.
* **Leverage workforce data** to anticipate hiring trends and business expansions.
* **Monitor competitive intelligence** to adapt strategies effectively.


## **Key Enrichments & Capabilities**

### **Firmographics Enrichment**

* Essential company details: revenue, employee count, industry classification.
* Use case: **Lead scoring, B2B marketing segmentation, sales intelligence.**

### **Technographics Enrichment**

* Insights into the internal tech stack of companies.
* Use case: **Competitive analysis, sales targeting, and software vendor tracking.**

### **Financial Metrics Enrichment**

* Historical and real-time financial indicators for publicly traded companies.
* Use case: **Investment research, risk assessment, financial modeling.**

### **Funding & Acquisitions Enrichment**

* Data on funding rounds, investors, M\&A activity.
* Use case: **VC investment analysis, startup tracking, competitive benchmarking.**

### **Strategic Insights & Competitive Landscape Enrichment**

* Insights into business strategies, operational focus, and competitive positioning.
* Use case: **Competitive intelligence, strategic business planning.**

### **Workforce Trends Enrichment**

* Analysis of department-level workforce changes and hiring trends.
* Use case: **HR analytics, market expansion strategies.**

### **Company Ratings Enrichment**

* Employee reviews, job satisfaction scores, and workplace ratings.
* Use case: **Employer branding, HR benchmarking, recruitment strategy.**

### **Business Challenges Enrichment**

* Identification of key risks, legal challenges, and operational issues faced by public companies.
* Use case: **Risk analysis, due diligence, financial modeling.**

### **Company’s Website Content Changes Enrichment**

* Detects **real-time updates** in company website content.
* Use case: **Brand monitoring, compliance tracking, competitive research.**

### **Keyword Search on Websites Enrichment**

* Searches for **specific keywords** across company websites.
* Use case: **Brand monitoring, product mentions, regulatory compliance.**

### **Company’s Social Media Presence Enrichment**

* Extracts insights from corporate social media activity.
* Use case: **Brand perception analysis, PR monitoring.**
