## Overview

Accessing your Explorium account is the first step in leveraging the powerful AI-driven data enrichment tools. Whether you're logging in for the first time or managing your credentials, this guide will help you navigate the process efficiently.

<Icon icon="thumbtack" size={22} color="red" iconType="solid"/> **Why is this important?**

- Ensures **secure and seamless access** to your Explorium account.
- Allows you to manage users and API access.
- Prevents access issues and potential security risks.

## How to Log In

### Step 1: Navigate to the Login Page

<Steps>
    <Step>
    Go to **[admin.explorium.ai](https://admin.explorium.ai/)**.
    </Step>
    <Step>
    Click on **Sign In**.
    </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 2: Enter Your Credentials

<Steps>
    <Step>
    Enter your **email address** and **password**.
    </Step>
    <Step>
    Click **Log In**.
    </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
 **Security Tip:**

 Enable **2FA** for added security.
</Tip>

## Creating a New Account

If you are a new user:

<Steps>
    <Step>
    Click **Sign Up** on the login page.
    </Step>
    <Step>
    Enter your **email address** and follow the instructions sent to your email.
    </Step>
    <Step>
    Set up your profile and **create a secure password**.
    </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
 **Note:**

 If your organization requires admin approval, your account may need verification before accessing the platform.
</Note>

## Troubleshooting Login Issues

If you experience login issues:

- **Wrong password?** Try resetting it using the **"Forgot Password?"** link.
- **Account locked?** Too many failed attempts may lock your account temporarily. Wait a few minutes and try again.
- **No verification email?** Check your spam folder or contact your **IT administrator**.

<Icon icon="envelope-dot" size={22} /> Need more help? Contact [support@explorium.ai](mailto:support@explorium.ai).
