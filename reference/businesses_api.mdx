## Overview

The **Explorium Business API** provides developers with a seamless way to **match, fetch, enrich, and analyze** business data at scale. Whether you're enhancing lead generation, optimizing risk assessment, or refining business intelligence, our API delivers **comprehensive, high-quality insights** to drive data-driven decisions.

### **Why use the Business API?**

- **Access an extensive business dataset** with coverage across **over 80 million companies worldwide**.
- **Leverage diverse firmographic, technographic, and financial insights** for better decision-making.
- **Automate business data enrichment** to eliminate manual research and improve efficiency.
- **Enhance decision-making** with real-time insights on company activities and trends.
- **Easily integrate with your CRM, analytics tools, and data pipelines for a unified workflow.**

<Icon icon="thumbtack"  color="red" iconType="solid"/> **Coverage at a Glance:**

- **80M+ businesses** across **150+ countries**.
- **Hundreds of firmographic attributes**, including company size, revenue, and industry classification.
- **Technographic data** covering thousands of technologies used by businesses.
- **Live tracking** of company growth, funding rounds, workforce changes, and key business events.

#### **Real-Time vs. Historical Data Considerations**

The Explorium Business API provides both **real-time event tracking** and **historical enrichment data**:

- **Event-Based APIs** surface only **recent business activities** (e.g., funding rounds, IPOs, hiring trends). Events are available **only for a defined timeframe (typically last 3 months)** and do not include historical data.
- **Enrichment APIs** provide **comprehensive historical data** on companies, including firmographics, technographics, and financials, which can be used for trend analysis and deeper business intelligence.
- For a **complete picture**, users can combine **event tracking with enrichment data**, enabling both **immediate insights** and **historical context** in their workflows.

## Key Capabilities

With the Explorium Business API, you can:

<Icon icon="check" /> **Match Businesses** – Accurately identify companies using name, domain, and multiple identifiers.
<Icon icon="check" /> **Retrieve Business Information** – Search and filter by location, industry, size, and other key attributes.
<Icon icon="check" /> **Enrich Business Profiles** – Add firmographic, technographic, and financial insights for deeper analytics.
<Icon icon="check" /> **Monitor Business Events** – Stay updated on funding rounds, IPOs, workforce changes, and M&A activity.
<Icon icon="check" /> **Autocomplete Business Fields** – Enable smarter search experiences with predictive autofill.

### **How It Works**

1. **Find and Match:** Start by searching for a business using a name, domain, or other identifier.
2. **Retrieve Core Details:** Get essential company information like industry, size, revenue, and headquarters.
3. **Enrich with Insights:** Layer on additional firmographic and technographic data for better profiling.
4. **Track Business Events:** Monitor real-time signals like leadership changes, new investments, or acquisitions.


## Best Practices for Using the Business API

- **Use multiple identifiers** (name, domain, tax ID) to improve match accuracy.
- **Regularly update enriched data** to maintain relevance.
- **Leverage real-time event tracking** to act on emerging business trends.
- **Optimize API queries** by refining filters to minimize redundant data pulls.
- **Use enrichment layers** to progressively deepen business insights.
- **Combine evens and enrichment data** for a complete company intelligence strategy.
