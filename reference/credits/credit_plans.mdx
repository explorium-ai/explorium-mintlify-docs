## Overview

To maximize your access to Explorium's services, you can **purchase additional credits** at any time. Whether you're scaling your API usage or need more data access, buying credits ensures uninterrupted operations.

<Icon icon="thumbtack" size={22} iconType="solid"/> **Why is this important?**

- Enables seamless expansion of API usage.
- Ensures continuous access to Explorium's data services.
- Provides flexibility to adjust credit consumption as needed.

## How to Purchase Additional Credits

### Step 1: Access the Pricing Page

<Steps>
    <Step>
    Log in to the **Explorium Admin Portal** at [admin.explorium.ai](https://admin.explorium.ai/).
    </Step>
    <Step>
    Navigate to **Pricing**.
    </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 2: Select a Credit Package

<Steps>
    <Step>
    View available **credit bundles** and their respective costs.
    </Step>
    <Step>
    Choose the package that fits your needs.
    </Step>
    <Step>
    Click **"Purchase Now"**.

    <Frame>
    ![](/images/reference/577d1ad11a5671f1e62e007c96f0f69fd5cef7a2d11573fe7cba45a57a07a118-image.png)
    </Frame>
    </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 3: Complete the Payment

- Enter **billing details** (if required).
- Choose a **payment method** (credit card, invoice, etc.).
- Confirm the purchase.

<Note>
 **Note:**

 Purchased credits are added to your account immediately after payment is processed.
</Note>

## Viewing Your Credit Balance

To check your updated credit balance:

<Steps>
    <Step>
    Navigate to **Overview** > **Consumption Report**.
    </Step>
    <Step>
    Review the available credits and usage history.
    </Step>
</Steps>

## Need Help?

For assistance with credit purchases or payment issues, contact [support@explorium.ai](mailto:support@explorium.ai).
